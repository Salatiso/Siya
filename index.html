Meet Siyabonga

Welcome! I'm Dr. Siyabonga Kala, an Anesthesiologist dedicated to providing the highest standard of patient care. My professional life is centered on precision, vigilance, and empathy, ensuring safety and comfort for those I serve.

Beyond the demanding yet rewarding field of medicine, I believe in a life filled with diverse interests and hands-on experiences. This space offers a glimpse into the passions that fuel my curiosity and provide balance to my life. From the thrill of a motorbike ride to the quiet satisfaction of a workshop project, these pursuits are an integral part of who I am.

I also cherish the time spent with my wife, Dr. Maggie, sharing life's adventures and supporting each other in our respective journeys.

Life's Adventures & Pursuits

Motorbiking

With a collection of about seven bikes, from agile superbikes to rugged dirtbikes, the open road and challenging trails offer an unmatched sense of freedom and focus. Each ride is an adventure.

DIY & Workshop

There's a unique satisfaction in understanding how things work. My workshop is my haven for tinkering, building, and meticulously fixing my own bikes – a true test of patience and precision.

Gardening

Connecting with nature through gardening brings a sense of peace and accomplishment. Nurturing plants from seed to bloom is a grounding counterpoint to a fast-paced life.

Travelling

Exploring new cultures, landscapes, and perspectives broadens the mind. Each journey, whether near or far, enriches my understanding of the world and its diverse wonders.

Basketball

The energy, strategy, and teamwork of basketball have always appealed to me. It’s a great way to stay active, engage in friendly competition, and appreciate collaborative effort.

Family & Connection

Sharing life's journey and passions with my wife, Dr. Maggie, brings immense joy and support to all my endeavors.
